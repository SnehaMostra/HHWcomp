ANNUAL MULTICULTURAL DANCE FESTIVAL

Conducted on: 24/8/2022

Preparation starts on: 4/8/2022

On August 24, 2022 SKS World School will presented its annual Multicultural Dance Festival. The program showcased the vibrant spirit of the diverse cultural communities of different Countries. Six styles of dance were represented: African, Russian, North Indian, South Indian, Chinese, and a lot more.
